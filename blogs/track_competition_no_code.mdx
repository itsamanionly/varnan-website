## Step 1: Track your competition with Social Listening Tools

Social Listening Tools feels like magic when we start using them. These tools track every tweet, post, blog, or press release based on your niche keywords and for your competitors. Think of it as Google Alerts on steroids.

For this demo, we will use **Kwatch.io** for social listening. Now we use this as this helps to track all our keywords from Reddit, Linkedin, Twitter at one place.

But here are some free alternatives to Kwatch that you can play around:

* **F5Bot** — Monitors Reddit and Hacker News keywords with email alerts
* **Reddit Comber** — Sends Reddit keyword notifications directly to your Reddit inbox
* **Notikey** — Tracks multiple subreddits with up to 15 keywords per monitor, plus email/webhook alerts
* **Google Alerts** — Watches the broader web (news, blogs, forums) for your keywords via email/RSS
* **Syften** — Alerts for mentions on Hacker News, Reddit, Stack Exchange, forums and blogs
* **CrowdLens** — AI-powered sentiment analysis across Reddit, Twitter (X), and LinkedIn

**Bonus Tip:** Don’t limit to just competitors. Track keywords, industry trends, new product launches — anything your startup lives or dies by.
